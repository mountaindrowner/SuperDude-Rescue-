Super Dude Danny — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Super Dude Danny does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

The game runs entirely on your device. It makes no network requests to any server, contains no advertising, no analytics or tracking, and no third-party SDKs. We do not know who you are, and we have no way to contact you or identify you.

What is stored on your device

The game saves your progress (which levels you have completed, best times, sound settings) in your device's local storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or anyone else. Deleting the app removes it.

Children's privacy

Super Dude Danny is suitable for children. Because the game collects no data of any kind, it collects no data from children. There are no accounts, no purchases, no chat, and no links that leave the game without an adult's involvement.

Contact

This game was built for The Crossroads Foundation. Questions about this policy can be directed to the ministry that distributes the app.
